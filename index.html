About

Telling
stories that
matter.

Documentary Film Photojournalism Conflict Analysis Humanitarian OSINT Pulitzer Center

I'm a documentary filmmaker, photojournalist, and humanitarian analyst based in London, with over a decade of field experience covering conflict, displacement, and human rights crises across Afghanistan, Ukraine, Palestine, Lebanon, and Europe.

My work has been published and broadcast by Al Jazeera, Scientific American, DW Documentary, Foreign Policy, Undark, The New Humanitarian, and others. I am a Pulitzer Center Crisis Reporting grant recipient.

Beyond filmmaking, I work as a conflict analyst and humanitarian adviser — with roles at Mercy Corps, INSO Afghanistan, and Safer Yemen — bringing an analytical depth to my storytelling that goes beyond the image.

I operate across the full production pipeline: scripting, solo field production, sound, editing, colour grading, motion graphics, and multilingual delivery. I shoot on Sony FX3 and fly DJI for aerial work.
